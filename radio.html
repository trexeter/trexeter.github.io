Internet Public Radio

Esto es RUTA - electronic music that embodies decentralized influences, cultural syncreti and contemporary club music, from deconstructed rhythms, heavy basslines, and ethereal vocals to breaks and creative sampling. We translate digital pieces into movement: dance! The third Sunday of every month at 21h Berlin Time (GMT+1)

Refuge Worldwide

RUTA takes over Refuge Worldwide for the second time. We starting the year fresh with a curation of residents and dear guests we look up to. Trexeter's solo debut on Refuge's booth. The first time he presented alongside María Vélez an ambient/bassy hybrid set, now fully on the decks he lays down a selection of fast paced and half speed deconstructed rhythms in a chilled radio format.

RUTA presents: Detour: First takeover at Refuge Worldwide to showcase an alternative selection of sounds, ranging from ambient, breaks, and more Andean-Caribbean experiments to refresh this summer Sunday. RUTA's residents Noise Casino & Trexeter, invite Colombian experimental artist María Vélez Gallo, Peruvian producer and co-founder of Casaloca Aristidez, and Chilean curator + mind behind Reverse Engineering DJ Glaucoma, for this Latinamerican sonic gathering.
