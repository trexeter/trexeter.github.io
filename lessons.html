With over 8 years of experience as a DJ and performer in the Berlin and Bogotá scenes, I offer personalized DJ lessons focused on both technical skills and artistic development.

The course is oriented towards electronic music across the spectrums of techno, bass, trance, breaks, house, electro, as well as more deconstructed forms such as ambient and sound art.

Lessons cover:

  • Hardware and software fundamentals
  • Music selection, curation, and library organization
  • Musical context and artistic conceptualization
  • Mixing techniques and styles
  • Headphone use, beatmatching, EQ and effects
  • Recording and structuring DJ sets

Lessons are adapted to the student’s level, interests, and artistic direction.
